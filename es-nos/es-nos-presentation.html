The Geocentric-Heliocentric Revolution

從地心說到日心說的革命

Explore the major shifts in humanity’s understanding of the universe—from Ptolemy’s geocentric model to Copernicus’s heliocentric theory—to gain a deeper understanding of the evolution of scientific knowledge, its historical context, and the core concepts underlying the nature of science.

Overall Course Objectives

課程總目標

Content (科學知識)

Describe the main components of the Ptolemaic (Geocentric) and Copernican (Heliocentric) models and explain why the phases of Venus served as critical falsifying evidence against the Ptolemaic system.

描述托勒密（地心說）和哥白尼（日心說）模型的主要內容，並解釋為什麼金星盈虧成為證偽托勒密系統的關鍵證據。

HOS (科學史)

Recognize the historical roles and contributions of key figures such as Ptolemy, Copernicus, Tycho Brahe, and Galileo in this paradigm shift.

認識托勒密、哥白尼、第谷和伽利略等關鍵人物在這場典範轉移中的歷史角色與貢獻。

NOS (科學本質)

Appreciate that scientific knowledge is tentative (changeable), empirically based, socio-culturally embedded, and involves human creativity. They will also be able to distinguish between scientific observation and inference.

體認科學知識是可變動的 (tentative)、植基於經驗證據 (empirically based)、受社會文化影響 (socio-culturally embedded)，並包含人類的創造力。學生也將能區分「科學觀察」與「科學推論」。

頁碼: 1

Module 1: The Perfect Universe

地心說模型

Content

The Ptolemaic Model

Introduction to "deferents" and "epicycles" as the mechanisms used to explain the observation of retrograde motion.

托勒密模型

介紹「均輪」(deferents) 與「本輪」(epicycles) 如何被用來「解釋」行星逆行 (retrograde motion) 的「觀測結果」。

HOS

Historical Context

Why did the Ptolemaic model dominate Western thought for over 1,400 years?

Philosophical Alignment

The model aligned perfectly with prevailing theological and philosophical views (i.e., the special, central place of Earth and humanity).

歷史背景：為什麼托勒密模型能主導西方思想超過 1400 年？

哲學契合：該模型與當時主流的神學和哲學觀點（即地球和人類的特殊、中心地位）完美契合。

NOS

The Function of a Scientific Model

How can a "wrong" model be so successful? (It was predictively powerful for its time).

科學模型的功能：一個「錯誤」的模型為何能如此成功？（它在當時具有「足夠強大」的預測能力）。

Science is Socio-Culturally Embedded

Scientific ideas are often accepted based on their fit with the dominant cultural framework.

科學受社會文化影響：科學觀點的被接受，常取決於它們是否符合當時的主流文化框架。

頁碼: 2

The Challenger (The Copernican Model)

哥白尼的日心說

Content

The Copernican (Heliocentric) Model

The Sun is at the center; Earth is one of several planets.

A More "Elegant" Explanation

How the model explains retrograde motion more simply (as Earth "overtaking" outer planets) without complex epicycles.

哥白尼（日心說）模型：太陽位於中心；地球只是眾多行星之一。

更「簡潔」的解釋：該模型如何更簡單地解釋逆行（地球「超越」運行較慢的外行星的結果），而不需要複雜的本輪。

HOS

Copernicus's Motivation

He was driven by a philosophical desire for a more "elegant" and "harmonious" system, not by new data.

Publication of De revolutionibus...

Why wait until his deathbed to publish (c. 1543)? Fear of ridicule and the intellectual climate.

哥白尼的動機：驅動他的是對一個更「簡潔」、「和諧」系統的哲學渴望，並非新的觀測數據。

《天體運行論》的出版：為何等到臨終前才出版（約 1543 年）？對當時學術氛圍和潛在嘲笑的擔憂。

NOS

Scientific Creativity

Copernicus's model was an act of profound imagination and creativity, re-imagining the same data.

科學的創造力：哥白尼的模型是一個深刻的「想像」與「創造」的行為，他只是用新的框架重新詮釋了相同的數據。

Simplicity

When two models explain the same data, scientists often prefer the simpler one.

簡潔性：當兩個模型都能解釋相同的數據時，科學家傾向於選擇較簡單的那個。

頁碼: 3

The Accumulation of Evidence

第谷與伽利略

Content

Tycho Brahe

The master of pre-telescopic observation, producing the most accurate data set of his time.

Galileo's Telescopic Discoveries

  1. The Moon's surface (imperfect)
  2. Sunspots (imperfect Sun)
  3. Moons of Jupiter
  4. Phases of Venus

第谷 (Tycho Brahe)：望遠鏡發明前的觀測大師，產出了當時最精確的天文數據集。

伽利略的望遠鏡四大發現：

  1. 月球表面（不完美）
  2. 太陽黑子（太陽不完美）
  3. 木星的衛星
  4. 金星的盈虧

HOS

Tycho's Role

Ironically, the "data-gatherer" did not accept the Copernican model; he proposed his own (Tychonic) model.

Galileo the "Advocate"

How Galileo used a new tool (the telescope) to gather new evidence and challenge the established order.

第谷的角色：諷刺的是，這位「數據收集者」並不接受哥白尼模型；他提出了自己的「第谷模型」（地日系統）。

伽利略作為「倡議者」：伽利略如何利用「望遠鏡」這個新工具來收集新證據，並挑戰既有權威。

NOS

Role of Technology

New technology (the telescope) can extend human senses and provide new empirical evidence previously inaccessible.

科技的角色：新科技（望遠鏡）可以「延伸人類的感官」，並提供以前無法獲得的、新的「經驗證據」。

Observation vs. Inference

Key distinction:

  • Observation: "Galileo saw points of light near Jupiter."
  • Inference: "He inferred they were moons."

關鍵區別：

  • 觀察：「伽利略看到木星旁邊有幾個亮點。」
  • 推論：「他『推斷』這些是木星的衛星。」
頁碼: 4

The Dialogue

跨越年代的科學對話

Content

CORE ACTIVITY: Modeling and Prediction

Prediction from Ptolemaic Model

Venus is locked between Earth and Sun.

Conclusion: It would never show a "gibbous" or "full" phase.

托勒密模型的預測：金星被鎖定在地球和太陽之間。

結論：地球上「永遠」不可能看到「盈凸月」或「滿月」相位的金星。

Prediction from Copernican Model

Venus orbits the Sun.

Conclusion: It will show a full set of phases (crescent, gibbous, full).

哥白尼模型的預測：金星繞著太陽轉。

結論：地球上將能看到金星「完整的盈虧相位」（包含眉月、盈凸月、滿月）。

The Decisive Evidence

Galileo observed the full set of phases.

決定性的證據：伽利略「觀測」到了完整的金星盈虧相位。

HOS

The "Smoking Gun"

This discovery was Galileo's "smoking gun" — the most powerful visual evidence against the Ptolemaic system.

Featured heavily in his Dialogue Concerning the Two Chief World Systems.

這項發現是伽利略的「冒煙的槍」（確鑿證據）——反對托勒密系統的最有力視覺證據。

這項證據在他的著作《關於兩大世界體系的對話》中佔有重要份量。

NOS

Falsifiability (Core NOS Concept)

Galileo's observation did NOT "prove" the Copernican model was correct (Tycho's model could also explain it).

However, it decisively "falsified" (disproved) the Ptolemaic model.

證偽性（本模組核心 NOS 概念）：伽利略的觀察並未「證明」哥白尼模型是對的（因為第谷模型也能解釋金星盈虧）。

然而，它決定性地「證偽」了托勒密模型。

Power and Limits of Evidence

Science progresses by disproving old models.

證據的力量與局限：科學的進展常常是透過「證偽」舊模型來達成的。
